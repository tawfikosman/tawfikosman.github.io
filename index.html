I am a second year PhD student at the University of Edinburgh, supervised by Professor Michael O’Boyle, Professor Amos Storkey, and Lecturer (Asst. Prof.) Elliot J. Crowley. I'm in the third year of the Pervasive Parallelism CDT programme.

My research interests include (but are not limited to!):

  • Architecture rewriting in neural networks
  • Optimising compilers
  • Program transformations

In my spare time I enjoy baking bread, climbing rocks, and using Oxford commas.
